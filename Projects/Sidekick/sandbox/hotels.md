# Hotels in South Delhi Serving Good Chinese Food

## 1. Jaypee Vasant Continental
- **Location:** Vasant Vihar, South Delhi (5‑star luxury hotel)  
- **Chinese Restaurant:** **Ano Tai** – fine‑dining specializing in authentic Schezwan and Cantonese cuisine  
- **Rating & Reviews:**  
  - Booking.com overall rating: **8.1/10** (Cleanliness 8.4, Comfort 8.4, Location 8.8, Facilities 8.1, Staff 8.6)  
  - KAYAK score: **3.9/5** (highly rated for service and amenities)  
  - Guest comment: “Extremely cooperative staff, very good service, clean rooms, dining option is great.” – Expedia review  
- **Notable Dishes:** Dim Sums, Mongolian Grill, Beijing Duck, Tofu Pepper & Salt  
- **Price Range:** Approx. **₹2,500 – ₹4,000 for two** (based on Zomato listings)  
- **Contact:** 011‑4607 2017  
- **Why Recommended:** Luxurious 5‑star setting, spacious rooms, excellent service, and a dedicated Chinese specialty restaurant with highly praised flavors.

## 2. The Oberoi, New Delhi – **Baoshuan**
- **Location:** New Delhi (central, easily accessible from South Delhi)  
- **Chinese Restaurant:** **Baoshuan** – modern Chinese cuisine and dim sum crafted under Michelin‑starred Chef Andrew Wong  
- **Rating & Reviews:**  
  - Featured in *Condé Nast Traveller India* Top 50 Restaurants list (2023 & 2025)  
  - Guest feedback highlights “limitless and exciting world of Chinese cuisine,” “heritage spanning 4,000 years.”  
- **Notable Dishes:** Region‑specific dishes from Tang dynasty temple cuisine to Shanghai jazz‑age cocktails; dim sum selection; signature Peking duck preparations.  
- **Price Range:** Premium fine‑dining; typical dinner for two **₹5,000 +** (exact pricing varies).  
- **Contact:** Check the Oberoi Hotels website or call the restaurant directly (phone not listed in snippet).  
- **Why Recommended:** Elite culinary experience with a menu inspired by China’s diverse regions, led by an internationally acclaimed chef.

## 3. The Claridges, New Delhi
- **Location:** Lutyens’ Delhi (close to South Delhi attractions)  
- **Chinese Restaurant:** **Jade** – renowned Chinese‑Cantonese restaurant offering a wide array of vegetarian and non‑vegetarian dishes  
- **Rating & Reviews:** Not individually scored in public APIs, but described as “renowned” and “culinary gem” on the hotel’s site.  
- **Notable Dishes:** Signature Cantonese classics, dim sum, specialty seafood and roast dishes.  
- **Price Range:** Mid‑to‑high‑end; typical banquet for two **₹3,000 – ₹5,000**.  
- **Contact:** Available on the Claridges website; front‑desk can arrange reservations.  
- **Why Recommended:** Elegant setting, attentive service, and a dedicated Chinese restaurant that emphasizes authentic Cantonese flavors.

## North India Restaurants (Additional Recommendations)

- **Karim’s (Old Delhi)** – Iconic Mughlai cuisine, famous for slow‑cooked biryanis and kebabs.  
- **Indian Accent (Lodhi Road, New Delhi)** – Contemporary Indian fine‑dining, frequently ranked among Asia’s best restaurants.  
- **Pind Balluchi (Multiple locations, Delhi NCR)** – Traditional Punjabi dishes served in a rustic setting.  
- **Bukhara (Maidens Hotel, New Delhi)** – Renowned for its Dal Bukhara and other North‑Indian specialties, part of the Taj group.  
- **Khan Chacha (Connaught Place, New Delhi)** – Popular for its chaat and North‑Indian street food snacks.  
- **Dishdasha (Various locations, Delhi NCR)** – Offers authentic Punjabi and Kashmiri cuisine with a focus on seasonal ingredients.  

*These restaurants are suggested for those looking to explore the broader North Indian culinary scene beyond South Delhi’s hotel‑based Chinese offerings.*
